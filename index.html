Brickell's Backpacking Checklist

What to Pack?

Planning a trip out into the woods? Don't know what to bring? Even the most experienced use a checklist to stay organized. This list will cover the basic essential items you'll need out on the trail.


Basic Gear

  • Backpack
  • Tent
  • Small Tarp
  • Sleeping Bag
  • Sleeping Pad
  • Map and Compass
  • Multi-Tool or Pocket Knife
  • Lighter
  • Headlamp
  • Water bottle
  • Sunscreen
  • Insect Repellent
  • Toilet Paper and Small Trowel
  • Small towel and Biodegradable Soap
  • Water Purifier or Filter
  • Portable Camp Stove
  • Extra Stove Fuel
  • Cookinng Set
  • Utensils
  • Plates and Bowels
  • Bear Bag or Bare-Proof Canister

Clothing

  • Base Layer
  • Hiking shirt
  • Hiking pants
  • Hiking boots
  • Hiking socks
  • Sandals
  • Mid Layer
  • Rain Jacket
  • Winter cap
  • Gloves
  • Gaiters
  • Sunglasses

First Aid

  • Anitseptic Wipes
  • Butterfly Bandages
  • Gause Pads
  • Fabric Bandages
  • Medical Tape
  • Blister Treatment
  • Pain-Relief Medications
  • Anti-Itch Treatment
  • Antihistamine
  • Tweezers
  • Safety Pins
  • First-Aid Manual
  • Liquid Bandage
  • Sunburn Relief
  • Antacid Tablets
  • Emergency Blanket
  • Signaling Mirror
  • Fire Steel and Tinder
  • Sewing Kit

Extras

  • Pari Cord
  • Metal Stakes
  • Camera
  • Batteries
  • Battery Pack
  • Saw
